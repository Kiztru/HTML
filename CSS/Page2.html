SELF-RESPECT

There is something else that can be included under respect which is self-respect. And the definition of it is “Pride and confidence in oneself; a feeling that one is behaving with honor and dignity.” It’s important because if you didn’t re-spect yourself you will not have the ability to respect others. Moreover, self-respect doesn’t mean that you have to respect yourself only, but it includes your respect towards animals, religions, cultures, believes, and governmental.


RESPECT EFFECT

SOCIAL LEVEL

Respect effect our life on social and personal level. In fact, treating people respectfully has many positive effects. Moreover, a respectful word has magical influence on people, it can make them happy, it gives them energy and confidant. Also it’s a very good way to make the discussions less aggressive, and make the other part except your opinion and beliefs without being angry or feeling hatred. Also on the social level, if you respect the government rules, your life will become easier without trespassing on individual rights, causing problems and committing crimes in the country


PERSONAL LEVEL

On the other hand, on the personal level, respecting others will be beneficial for yourself and your career. Moreover, it will help expanding your world, your reputation will precede you, you will feel the inner peace, and as they always say “respect to be respected.”
